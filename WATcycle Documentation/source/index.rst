WATcycle: Water Analysis Tool for Terrestrial Water Cycle and Water Budget
====================================
A comprehensive toolbox for performing advanced hydrological analysis, including data preprocessing, statistical calculations, and visualizations. This repository contains Jupyter Notebooks organized into folders and subfolders. The development of such a resource is intended to enable users in conducting their hydrological research and extend it to their relevant activities.
